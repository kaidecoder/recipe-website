Curry Goat

Description

In Jamaica, curry goat isn’t the ‘every Sunday’ type of dish. More expensive than chicken or cow meat, it’s reserved for special occasions such as weddings, parties, fun fairs, and funerals. It is goat meat marinade in Jamaican spices and slow cooked in a savoury curry sauce

Ingredients

  • Cinnamon
  • Ginger
  • Clove
  • Sugar
  • Thyme
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Allspice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Goat pieces
  • Curry Powder

Steps

  1. Wash the goat meat with vinegar and lime/lemon juice before adding the seasoning. The lime and vinegar will help cut the odour from the meat.
  2. Traditionally, the goat meat is marinated for 24 hours to maximise the curry flavour. If your time is limited, marinate for at least 2 hours.
  3. Allow the goat to slowly cook until tender.
